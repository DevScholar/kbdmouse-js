Notice: The virtual mouse's logging function is only available on mobile devices.
Use `npm run dev:expose` to expose the webpage to the local network
(for example, connect your computer to your phone's hotspot and open the webpage on your phone).
Moving your finger without long pressing represents mouse movement.
After a long press, moving your finger represents drag mode (left mouse button pressed and mouse movement).
After a long press, keeping your finger still represents a right-click.
Single and double-click events are also supported.
